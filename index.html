Jiangtao Li（李江涛）

  • Email: lijiangtao AT shu.edu.cn
  • Office: J423, SCES

    • I am a Lecturer at the School of Computer Engineering and Science at Shanghai University. My research interests are in Cybersecurity and public-key cryptography. I got my Ph.D. from East China Normal University, China (Under the supervision of Zhenfu Cao and Lei Zhang). During my Ph.D., I visited ENS de Lyon, France (with Benoit Libert and Junqing Gong). I also spent half a year as a Senior Research Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (with Professor Kwok-Yan Lam).

    Today, my research focuses mainly on security and privacy issues in the Internet of Vehicles. I am also working on the theory of group signatures and functional encryption.
