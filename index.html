Jiangtao Li（李江涛）

I am a Lecturer at the School of Computer Engineering and Science at Shanghai University. My research interests are in Cybersecurity and public-key cryptography. I got my Ph.D. from East China Normal University, China (Under the supervision of Zhenfu Cao and Lei Zhang). During my Ph.D., I visited ENS de Lyon, France (with Benoit Libert and Junqing Gong). After PhD, I spent half a year as a Senior Research Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (with Professor Kwok-Yan Lam).

Today, my research focuses mainly on security and privacy issues in the Internet of Vehicles. I am also working towards efficient and useful functional encryption.
